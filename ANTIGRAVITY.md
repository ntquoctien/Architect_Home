# Antigravity Agent Rules
- **Workflow:** Spec-Driven Development (OpenSpec).
- **Source of Truth:**
    - Specs: `Docs/OpenSpecs/changes/`
    - Memory: `Docs/Memory/`
- **Constraint:** "No Spec, No Code". Trước khi sửa bất kỳ file .cs nào, phải kiểm tra `Docs/OpenSpecs/changes/tasks.md`.
- **Memory Architecture:** Tuân thủ thiết kế Semantic & Episodic trong `Docs/OpenSpecs/changes/design.md`.
